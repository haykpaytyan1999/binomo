CHOOSING THE RIGHT TIME FOR TRADING

Binomo uses the Fixed Time Trade (FTT) system. This system requires you to forecast where the market will move. If you have a prediction that the market will go up, you will open a trading position "Up" or up, and vice versa. There are two types of FTT, including: Short-term trading has a time limit of less than 5 minutes. Long-term trading has a time limit of 15 to 60 minutes.

You can change the time frame by clicking the round icon in the lower left corner of the chart. The time frame depends on your trading style.

Many people think of Binomo login as a game.
